Our Process

  • 1

    Initial Exploration and Deep Questioning

    In this first step, Maieutica delves into the proposed technological idea. The company's experts guide teams through fundamental questions that explore the bigger picture.
  • 2

    Strategic Breakdown and Detailed Analysis

    Once the idea has been explored from a broad perspective, Maieutica makes a strategic breakdown. The essential components that will make up the technological solution are analyzed.
  • 3

    Iteration, Validation, and Continuous Improvement

    After the idea has been broken down and defined, we collaborate with the client to develop functional prototypes and proof of concepts to assess the effectiveness of the solution
